ManasDB core is licensed under Apache 2.0 + Commons Clause.

## What Is Free — Forever

The following uses are free with no license required:

✅ Building any application or product that uses ManasDB
as a dependency (regardless of whether your product
is commercial or generates revenue)

✅ Using ManasDB in production at your company
as part of your internal tooling or customer-facing product

✅ Open source projects of any kind

✅ Research, education, and personal use

✅ Modifying ManasDB for your own internal use

## What Requires A Commercial License

A commercial license is required if your use falls into
any of the following categories:

❌ Selling a product whose primary value is ManasDB
itself — not what you built using ManasDB

❌ Repackaging ManasDB with minor modifications and
selling or distributing it as your own product

❌ Offering any hosted, managed, or cloud-delivered
vector memory or RAG retrieval service to third parties
where ManasDB is a core component of the service

❌ Rebranding or substantially repackaging ManasDB
and distributing it as a competing SDK or library,
whether for free or for fee

## The Simple Test

Ask yourself:
"Am I selling ManasDB, or am I selling something
I built using ManasDB?"

If you built something WITH ManasDB → Free. Always.
If you are selling ManasDB itself → License required.
